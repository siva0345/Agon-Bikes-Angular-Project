KTM RC-200

Engine:The KTM RC 200's liquid-cooled, single-cylinder, 4-stroke engine with a displacement of 199.5cc delivers impressive power and torque, ensuring thrilling acceleration and responsiveness.

Performance: With approximately 25 horsepower and 19 Nm of torque, the KTM RC 200 offers exhilarating performance ideal for navigating urban streets and enjoying spirited rides on highways.

Design:Its aggressive styling, coupled with an upright riding position and ergonomic handlebar-footpeg configuration, not only exudes sportiness but also ensures rider comfort and maneuverability in urban traffic and on twisty roads.

Features: The KTM RC 200 boasts a liquid-cooled 199.5cc engine, precise fuel injection, advanced suspension, reliable disc brakes, sporty design, digital instrumentation, customizable color options, and a range of available accessories.

Suspension and Brakes:The KTM RC 200 features advanced suspension with upside-down telescopic forks at the front and a monoshock absorber at the rear, coupled with reliable disc brakes for precise and responsive handling.

Price: The Ex-Showroom price of KTM RC 200 is Rs.2,14,694.It is positioned as an entry-level sports bike, offering an attractive combination of performance, style, and affordability.

The KTM RC 200 is a dynamic sports bike featuring a 199.5cc engine, aggressive design, precise handling, and modern technology such as a digital instrument cluster and ABS, offering an exhilarating riding experience.
