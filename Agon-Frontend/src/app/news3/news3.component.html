2023 KTM 390 ADVENTURE launched in India with Fully Adjustable Suspension & Spoke Wheels

15 May 2023

Fully adjustable WP APEX suspension. Front USD forks for both compression & rebound dampening. Rear mono-shock for pre-load adjustability and rebound dampening.

Heavy-duty aluminium spoke wheels. 19” front and 17” rear with black anodized aluminium rims

Rally-inspired graphics. Aggressive look inspired by KTM's vast Dakar Rally racing heritage

Intelligent rider aids. MTC (Motorcycle Traction Control), Ride modes (off-road & street), Cornering ABS, Off-road ABS, Quickshifter+, TFT (Thin-Film Transistor) display, My Ride connectivity

Priced at Rs. 3.60 Lacs (Ex-Showroom, Delhi)

Pune, May 15, 2023: KTM, the World’s largest & India’s fastest-growing premium European brand, announced its 2023 KTM 390 ADVENTURE model at a price of Rs. 3.60 Lacs (Ex-Showroom, Delhi)

What's New in the 2023 KTM 390 Adventure?

Feature Description
Fully Adjustable Suspension WP APEX suspension hardware is fully adjustable for compression, rebound, and preload.
Spoke Wheels Equipped with tough yet light spoked wheels (19” front and 17” rear) with black anodized aluminium rims.
