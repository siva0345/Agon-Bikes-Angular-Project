About Triumph

The new Triumph Speed 400, recently launched in India, embodies the epitome of modern classic design and performance. Crafted in Hinckley, UK, and manufactured by Bajaj Auto Ltd. in its Chakan plant, this roadster boasts a potent all-new engine with a six-speed gearbox, delivering 40 PS peak power and 37.5 Nm peak torque. Loaded with advanced features like traction control, ride-by-wire throttle, and torque-assist clutch, it promises an exhilarating ride. With its iconic silhouette, premium finishes, and rider-focused technology, the Speed 400 sets a new standard in the realm of classic motorcycles, catering to both seasoned riders and newcomers alike.
