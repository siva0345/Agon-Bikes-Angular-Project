Engine: The Bajaj Pulsar N160 is equipped with a powerful 160cc engine, delivering impressive performance and efficiency.

Performance: With a maximum power output of 16.5 hp and torque of 14.6 Nm, the N160 offers dynamic acceleration and excellent fuel economy.

Design: Sporting a sleek and aerodynamic design, the N160 features striking graphics, sharp lines, and modern styling elements, ensuring a captivating presence on the road.

Features: Bajaj has equipped the N160 with advanced features, including a digital instrument cluster, LED lighting, alloy wheels, and a comfortable seating position, enhancing both style and functionality.

Suspension and Brakes: The bike comes with telescopic front forks and a mono-shock rear suspension setup, providing superior comfort and stability, while its front and rear disc brakes ensure precise and responsive braking performance.

Price: The Ex-Showroom price of the Bajaj Pulsar N160 varies depending on the country and optional features chosen. Positioned competitively in the commuter segment, it offers excellent value for money, making it an attractive choice for riders seeking a blend of performance and affordability.
