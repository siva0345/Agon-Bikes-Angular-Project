Engine: The Dominar 400 is powered by a single-cylinder, liquid-cooled, 373.3cc engine. This engine is capable of delivering a healthy amount of power and torque, making it suitable for both city commuting and highway cruising.

Performance: The engine produces around 40 horsepower and 35 Nm of torque, providing strong acceleration and good overall performance. It's known for its smooth power delivery across the rev range.

Design: The Dominar 400 features a sporty and muscular design, with a full LED lighting system for enhanced visibility. Its design elements give it a distinctive look compared to other motorcycles in its segment.

Features: One of the highlights of the Dominar 400 is its feature-rich package. It comes equipped with a fully digital instrument cluster that provides information such as speed, rpm, fuel level, gear position, and more. Additionally, it features dual-channel ABS (Anti-lock Braking System) for improved safety.

Comfort: The Dominar 400 is designed with rider comfort in mind. It features a comfortable riding position with an upright handlebar and well-padded seats, making it suitable for long rides.

Suspension and Brakes: The Dominar 400 comes with a telescopic front fork and a monoshock rear suspension setup, offering a good balance between comfort and handling. It also features disc brakes at both ends for efficient braking performance.

Price: One of the appealing factors of the Dominar 400 is its affordability. It offers a good value proposition for riders looking for a capable and feature-packed motorcycle without breaking the bank.

Overall, the Bajaj Dominar 400 is a popular choice among riders looking for a versatile and value-for-money motorcycle that excels in various riding conditions, from daily commuting to long-distance touring.
